I would like to welcome everyone to my personal world of development. This is my canvas of development. Hello everyone, my name is Nicholas “Nu” Perry, and I am a 29-year-old self-taught jr. web developer. I have been on and off with programming for the last 2 years and decided to take it seriously in 2021. I made the decision to take it seriously because I began to see the potential that programming had to create beautiful pieces of art. I am a huge fan of music, anime, video games, fashion, and tech. Some of my hobbies include, playing baseball, cooking, playing games at semi competitive level, playing chess and watching anime with my kids. One day I want to have the ability to create any app, website, or video game for people to enjoy one day. I want to thank everyone for taking time to check out my website.
